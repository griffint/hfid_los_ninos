Welcome to Roomie. Our goal is to help people like you find roommates to live with and places for you to live. Below are some tips for first time users.


Use the map view to find people and places in the areas you want to live!

Groups provide a way to hunt for a house or apartment with a group of people that you want to live with!

Check out someone's badges to see the ways they're verified, or things you have in common with them!
